Outdoor Furniture

Modular set of outdoor furniture

Project description

Make a set of mainly wooden furniture for the outdoors. The project was very open, but the client wanted the furniture to help people to gather and enjoy the outdoors.

Ideation

I started out by analyzing the location the funiture would be used. The backyard was small, and gray.

I then started sketching different ideas for the furniture. I wanted the furniture to be modular, so that it could be used in many different ways. I also wanted the furniture to be colorful, so that it would stand out in the backyard.

Prototyping

Small scale

I prototyped first in cardboard to test different shapes and ideas, quickly and cheap.

Then I mooved over to 3D printing to test different legs and different angles, and quickly swap parts.

Large scale

I mooved over to wood to test the final design and material. I tried to make the entire bench/chair out of wood, since I don't have any experience with wielding steel. I liked the look of the design with steel legs better, so the final design is a mix of wood and steel.

The final product

Concept

Consisting of a chair, a bench and a table with an angle of 30°, this set of outdoor furniture can be put together in many different ways.

If there is a concert the furniture can be placed in a line and for the everyday social situation, the furniture can be placed in more circular shapes to encourage socialization.

The colorful legs compliment the playfulness of the shape that can be created with the different parts.

Material

Pine wood and powder coated stainless steel.
