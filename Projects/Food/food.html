The Incredible Journey of Food

A cookbook for children

Background

With a new generation of children growing up in urban areas, there is a challenge to decrease the cognitive distance to what goes into food production. Insight into foods origin, production of foodstuffs and the road from food to meal is on the National Curriculum as of kindergarten. Seeing as dietary habits that are established at a young age, are most often continued later in life; it is beneficial to address this at an early age. Giving children the knowledge and tools for critical thinking about the food they eat can have impact on their carbon footptint.

Methods

The main method of acquiring information was through literature studies, as well as exploring existing products and possibilities. We had originally arranged for a meeting with a kindergarten to gather first-hand experiences and further ahead do focus groups and test concepts with children. Unfortunately, the Corona-outbreak rendered this method futile. Pursuing the commercial market, we turned to even further literature studies, a digital survey, as well as our own experiences with cookbooks and children.

Users and distribution

The primary users is children between 3 and 10 years of age. Children are highly impressionable, and habits that are established at a young age are often continued later in life. Seeing as most often it is the parents who have the biggest influence on their children, and responsible for purchases, they are the target buyers and secondary users.

The book will be distibuted through book stores both online and physical. One can envision several types of marketing, both targeted ads through social media and collaborations with renowned names and organizations.

Our goal

Our goal is that the book is going to be more than just a regular recipe book. We want it to start a conversation about sustainability and food between the child and their parents. It is therefore envisaged that the child and their parents will do several of the tasks together, so they can learn and explore in fellowship. In addition, we want the book to plant a seed of consciousness in the child. This is done by introducing the child to information, tasks, and facts they can reflect upon. Not least, we want to encourage to activity. Therefore, we are including an activity for every recipe of the book. The goal is that the activities shall help create an understanding of the relationship between food and sustainability in the child.

Value and impact

Through practicing food sustainability, the children will be empowered to explore values, construct knowledge, and develop an appreciation of the environment and the food they are eating. From a climate perspective, it is of great value to learn children about environmental issues connected to food. This will make them think about the consequences of their food choices and behavior. What we are teaching children today is going to affect their lifestyle choices in the future. Giving children the knowledge and tools for critical thinking about the food they eat, can make a big impact on both greenhouse gas-emissions and public health, since a person's diet and food choices makes up a large part of their carbon footprint.

The final product

Goal

Each recipe is tailored to convey knowledge about one or more goal. They are seasonality, dietary choices impact on climate, growing, animal welfare and local produce. This recipe aims to give a consciousness about the dietary choices’ impact on climate by means of resource utilization.

Layout and graphics

The layout and graphics aim to be colorful, playful and familiar to children. The hand drawn lines, illustrations and freeform shapes creates a playful feeling. At the same time, the colors, symbols and frames stays coherent throughout the recipes, which makes each page familiar and intuitive to understand.

Simple recipes

The intention is that children can partake actively in cooking with their parents. The recipes are simplified and instructions written so that they are easy to follow by children. Even so, some steps would likely require guidance from adults, which makes for teachable moments between parent and child.

Background information

To further the learning outcome, background information is presented on ingredients, underlying foodsystems etcetera. The topics that come up can be comprehensive and difficult, thus great effort is put into creating examples and illustrations that intuitively convey the message.

Activities

Each recipe has an activity besides cooking linked to it. The activities are made so that the children better engage with the book, hopefully sparking creativity and joy. For example in this recipe, a slider is embedded in the page, so the child can pull on and reveal the difference. In other recipes we have seed paper you can rip out and plant, a map to mark out the foods origin, draw a line between the right animal and product and so on.

Book series

The book series is called The incredible journey of food, which is in reference to the food system as well as the process of cooking food and creating something with different foods. In this book the topic is «foods you know - from scratch». The recipes is based on processed foods and how to make them from scratch, such as: ketchup. pasta, jam and fish fingers. In other editions of the book the topics could be: seasonality, regions of the world, favorite dinners etc.
