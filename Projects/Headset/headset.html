Clear lines and big shapes

Project description

Make a set of mainly wooden furniture for the outdoors. The project was very open, but the client wanted the furniture to help people to gather and enjoy the outdoors.

Concept

Consisting of a chair, a bench and a table with an angle of 30Â°, this set of outdoor furniture can be put together in many different ways.

If there is a concert the furniture can be placed in a line and for the everyday social situation, the furniture can be placed in more circular shapes to encourage socialization.

The colorful legs compliment the playfulness of the shape that can be created with the different parts.

The user

The target user is children in elementary school, and secondary users are everyone else. From doing some research on items designed for children, the key qualitie the pencil sharpener should have are:

  • Vibrant colors
  • Easy to use
  • Not to big (children have small hands)

    • Prototyping

    Shape

    The pencil sharpener is in the shape of a cylinder. This makes it comfortable to hold in your hand. The elastomer also makes it more comfortable to hold and gives a good grip when twisting the pencil. In addition, the elastomer gives the pencil sharpener a softer look and feel. It also allows a pop of color that gives it a more playful look.

    The cone is also natural to pull on to open the pencil sharpener to empty it. The lid is over-molded in elastomer, which makes it possible to close the pencil sharpener with friction between the lid and the container.

    The prototyping was done by 3D-modeling in Fusion 360. This projcet was done during the pandemic, and access to other prototyping methods was limited.

    Size

    The pencil sharpener should fit comfortably in children's hands, and it is therefore 44mm tall and 40mm in diameter.

    Material
