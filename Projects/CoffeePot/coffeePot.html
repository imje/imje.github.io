Coffe Pot

An aesthetics project

Project description

The project was to create a coffe pot using a watering can as a totem. You should be able to tell from the different parts of the pot what they are used for.

Ideation

The parts

A coffe pot is made up of several parts that shoud each tell a story about what they are used for.

The handel should signal that it is for holding.

The spout should signal that it is for pouring.

The container should signal that it is for storing.

The top should signal that it is for pouring in liquid.

The handle

The handle is anglet downwards to show a natural grabbing angle for a hand, and to ease pouring. The triangle shape fits well into a hand, and the edges are rounded to make it comfortable to hold.

The top

The top is shaped like a cone with the large opening at the top to signal that it is a opening for pouring in liquid. And since it is smaller at the bottom, it signals that the liquid will flow downwards into the container. The larger opening at the top also eases pouring into the pot.

The container

The container is a cylinder with a rounded bottom to signal that it is for storing liquid. It showns that the containeris solid and stable.

The spout

The spout is a continuation of the handle, and has a opening at the end so show the liquid flowing out. The continuation of the handle signals that it is the end of the cooffee pot "ritual". You start with the handle, then the top and container, and end with the spout. The triangle shape of the spout also signals that the liquid will flow downwards.

The prototype

Material

We used painted foam and a 3D-printed part.

Shape and color

We rounded all the edges to make the shape softer so that it looks more comfortable to hold and use. We also chose the matte blue color to soften the pot.
